Collaboration & Influence

  1. To what extent does your organization have a well-defined digital or IT strategy?

    (1 = No strategy, 5 = Clearly defined, communicated, and widely adopted strategy, )

  2. How centralized is your IT or digital governance model?

    (1 = Fully decentralized, 3 = Federated, 5 = Fully centralized)

  3. How comprehensive and usable are your enabling artefacts (e.g., policies, playbooks, tech radars)?

    (1 = None or ad hoc, 5 = Complete, up-to-date and used actively in decision-making)

Value & Impact

  1. How predictable and stable is your business model in the current geopolitical climate?

    (1 = Highly volatile, 5 = Stable with low impact from external geopolitical factors)

  2. How close is your business to the end consumer?

    (1 = B2B with long value chains, 5 = Direct-to-consumer relationship)

  3. To what extent does geopolitics impact your product pricing or service delivery?

    (1 = Strong impact on pricing/supply chain, 5 = Minimal or no impact)

  4. How culturally diverse and globally integrated is your organization?

    (1 = Local, homogeneous culture, 5 = Global, highly diverse culture)

  5. How mature is your organization in terms of age and adaptability?

    (1 = Over 100 years and slow to change, 5 = Younger or highly agile despite age)

  6. How would you rate your Net Promoter Score (NPS) trend over the last 5 years?

    (1 = Significant decline, 5 = Consistent improvement)

  7. How has your NPS changed over the last 10 years?

    (1 = Declined consistently, 5 = Strong, long-term upward trend)

  8. How frequently does your organization engage in mergers, acquisitions, carve-outs, or ecosystem collaborations?

    (1 = Rarely or never, 5 = Actively and regularly as part of strategy)

Mandate & Power

  1. How effective are your digital governance structures (e.g., councils, committees, decision boards)?

    (1 = None exist, 5 = Multiple well-functioning governance bodies with clear mandates)

  2. Where does the CDO role report within the organization?

    (1 = Deep in IT or middle management, 5 = Directly to CEO or board)

Culture & Change Readiness

  1. How courageous is your leadership in making bold, risky decisions for transformation?

    (1 = Risk-averse and defensive, 5 = Bold and transformative)

  2. How much compassion and empathy does leadership consistently show to employees?

    (1 = Very low, 5 = Actively nurtured and visible in policies/actions)

  3. How often has the organization undergone structural changes (reorgs) in the last 5 years?

    (1 = Constant reorganizations every year, 5 = Rare and strategically driven changes)

  4. How frequently have the company’s Vision, Mission, and Values (VMV) changed in the last 5 years?

    (1 = Changed almost yearly, 5 = Clear and stable)

  5. How frequently have the VMVs changed in the last 10 years?

    (1 = Multiple shifts, 5 = Minor or no changes with sustained relevance)

  1. How resilient is your organization‚Äôs cybersecurity posture (e.g., breaches in the last 5 years)?

    (1 = Multiple serious incidents, 5 = No significant incidents)

  2. How digitally native is your company in terms of product and process design?

    (1 = Legacy-driven, 5 = Born digital or fully re-platformed)

  3. What is the age of the oldest core system still in production use?

    (1 = 25+ years, 5 = All systems modernized or under 5 years old)

  4. To what extent does cross-border data transfer affect your business operations?

    (1 = Critical blocker, 5 = Minimal impact)
